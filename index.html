About Us

"Perks & Passions: The History of 'Cozy Cup'"

Discover the story behind CozyCup Café...

"In the heart of a bustling urban neighborhood, "Cozy Cup" began as a modest coffee shop in the 1950s. Founded by Serge and Kenneth, it evolved over the decades into an artistic haven in the 1960s, an artisanal gem in the 1980s, and a digital-age hotspot in the 2000s. During the pandemic in the 2020s, it adapted, supporting the community. Today, run by their grandchildren, it's a living legacy, a place where the love for coffee and community lives on, one cup at a time."
